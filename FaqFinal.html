Frequently Asked questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at Mantech Helpdesk is required. This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

How much time does it take for registration request to process?

Usually it take 24 hrs to process the registration request.But in some cases it may take 2-3 days.

Is there any registration fees?

No, registration is absolutely free.USD
Complaint

Who can raise an issue?

Any employee, who is registered on the portal can raise an issue with the Mantech Helpdesk.

I want to raise an issue - what are the steps?

The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Register an account
  • Activate your account
  • Go to the Raise an issue section and report your complaint
  • A technician will be assigned to your issue.
  • Your issue will be solved within the given time.

Can I report multiple issues at a time?

No, only single issue should be reported at a time. However multiple complaints can be raised for different issues.
